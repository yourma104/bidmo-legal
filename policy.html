    • Showing personalized ads via Google AdMob
    • Accessing your device location
    • Sending you push notifications
    • Accessing your media files (e.g., for uploading photos or videos)
  • Contractual Necessity (Art. 6(1)(b))
    We process your data when it is required to fulfill our agreement with you, including:
    • Creating and managing your account
    • Enabling bidding, listing, and messaging features
    • Delivering core app functionality and services
  • Legitimate Interest (Art. 6(1)(f))
    We may process certain non-sensitive data to:
    • Improve the app’s performance and usability
    • Ensure platform security and prevent misuse
    • Collect aggregated usage trends (if not tied to identifiers)
  • Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))
    In limited cases, we are legally required to process data to:

    • Respond to official legal or regulatory requests

Additional Data & Permissions

Location Access
With your consent, we use your device's location to show nearby content or tailor app features. We only access your approximate city-level location and do not track your real-time movement. This data is processed via Geolocator and Geocoding plugins.

Media and File Access
You may choose to upload files (e.g., profile pictures, listings). If you do, we use tools like File Picker, Image Picker, Image Cropper, and Flutter Image Compress to allow media selection, editing, and optimization before upload. This content stays local unless you actively upload it.

Crash and Performance Reporting
With your consent, we use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymized crash logs and device data to identify and fix issues. This helps improve the stability of BidOn.

App Verification
We use Firebase App Check to protect the platform from unauthorized or harmful activity. It ensures that data requests originate from the legitimate app. No personal data is collected through this process.

Our app may display photos, videos, or GIFs uploaded by users or from external sources. No additional data is collected when viewing media.
